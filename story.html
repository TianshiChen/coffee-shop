Our Story

Design concept

  • The design advocates advocating natural ecology, return to nature, not refined.
  • Interior display focuses on sunlight, simplicity, freshness and atmosphere
  • Furniture items focus on the "vegetarian" school, flowers, squares, linen, wooden seats, etc.
  • Interior focus on natural performance and greenery integration

Decoration Style

  • The main style of rustic style is to advocate the return to nature.
  • The use of tiles with the ability to harvest rustic feelings, is not more meaningful.
  • Elegant decoration to create a pure rustic style.
  • A variety of cloth broken flower pattern has a natural and intimate feeling.
  • Printed with large square pattern curtains, low-key show the rustic atmosphere, which is the unique charm of the square pattern.
  • The fresh tender green style decoration is as elegant and quiet as a picture, showing the casual and romantic life of the rustic style.
