Caffè Latte

This is a traditional classic drink - espresso blended into hot milk, which is covered with a light layer of milk
froth. When tasting this coffee, you can choose to add a special flavor (such as vanilla, caramel or almond
flavor) to the syrup.

Caffè Mocha

This coffee is made with a mellow mocha sauce, espresso and steamed milk blended together and topped with
whipped cream. In the cold days, in the sad times, no one can resist her temptation.

Cappuccino

This coffee is made by our skilled baristas, following the traditional technique of gently pouring hand-made hot milk
and delicate milk froth over the espresso.

Espresso

This is the essence of coffee, in its most concentrated form. Espresso with a touch of caramel flavor, rich and fragrant.
